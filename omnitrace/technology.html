Plug-and-play installation

The OT-1 clips onto the ZASS vehicle's CAN port under the overhead guard. No harness surgery, no firmware changes — 15 minutes per vehicle, tools included.

Real-time data acquisition

The unit passively reads every frame on the vehicle network — battery, motors, hydraulics, steering — sampling 200+ parameters continuously without touching vehicle behaviour.

3

Secure cloud transmission

4G/LTE uplink wrapped in TLS 1.3, payloads encrypted with AES-256. Telemetry lands in the OmniTrace cloud in under 200 ms — anywhere on earth. Offline? The OT-1 buffers 72 hours locally and syncs on reconnect.

Your command center

Live maps, battery DNA, fault feeds and OTA control — rendered at 120 fps in the Telemetry dashboard, with alerts that find you before problems find your fleet.
